# The Calling

The player is a servant in the Broken Wheel tavern, at the edge of the
border town of Millhaven. They have known nothing but scrubbing floors
and serving ale their entire life.

One evening, a weathered knight (Sir Aldric) and an ancient mage (Fenris)
enter the tavern. They have been searching for someone with the mark of
a prophecy. They offer coins to Marta, the tavern keeper, for the
servant's freedom.

Outside, bandits who have been tracking the knight attack. This forces
the first combat encounter, where Aldric teaches the player to fight.

Tone: Dark fantasy, grounded. The danger is real.
